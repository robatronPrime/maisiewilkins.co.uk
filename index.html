About

Maisie Wilkins is a womanswear designer with a strong interest in pattern cutting. She finds satisfaction in the process of creating a garment; finding the right fit and crafting something that complements and extends the human body.

Her methods are very hands on, often using garments to drape on the stand, distorting everyday silhouettes and allowing them to be seen with new eyes. She also enjoys working with basic shapes on the stand, by deconstructing and reconstructing them to create new silhouettes.
